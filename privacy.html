Enterprise Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

At Hashvex Technologies LLC, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information that applies to our Bitcoin mining platform, and your choices about the collection and use of your information. By using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Bitcoin mining platform, including:

1.1 Personal Identification Information

When you create an account or use our mining services, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Government-issued identification for KYC verification (e.g., passport, driver's license)
  • Proof of address documents (utility bills, bank statements)
  • Selfie or video verification for identity confirmation
  • Business documentation for corporate accounts

1.2 Financial and Mining Information

To process mining investments and withdrawals, we collect:

  • Bitcoin wallet addresses for payouts
  • Mining transaction history and investment records
  • Bank account details (for fiat transactions where applicable)
  • Tax identification numbers where required by law
  • Mining hardware allocation and performance data

1.3 Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform:

  • IP address, browser type, and device characteristics
  • Operating system and system configuration
  • Pages visited, clickstream data, and session duration
  • Referring website addresses and exit pages
  • Mining dashboard interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and secure your mining account
  • Service Delivery: To process mining investments, Bitcoin payouts, and other transactions
  • Compliance: To verify identity and meet regulatory obligations (AML/KYC) for mining operations
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and malicious mining activities
  • Customer Support: To respond to inquiries and resolve technical mining issues
  • Platform Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and enhance mining platform functionality
  • Communications: To send important notices, mining updates, and service-related announcements
  • Performance Monitoring: To track mining hardware performance and optimize operations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

3.1 Storage Infrastructure

All user data is stored in enterprise-grade infrastructure with the following protections:

  • Data encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Network isolation through VPC peering and private subnets
  • Regular vulnerability scanning and penetration testing
  • Automated backups with point-in-time recovery capabilities
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant data centers

3.2 Authentication Security

We use JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure authentication:

  • Tokens signed with a strong secret key (JWT_SECRET)
  • Default token expiration (JWT_EXPIRES_IN) set to 24 hours
  • Refresh token implementation for extended sessions
  • Multi-factor authentication mandatory for all accounts
  • Biometric authentication support for mobile access

3.3 Additional Protections

  • All communications encrypted with TLS 1.3
  • Regular security audits and independent code reviews
  • Strict access controls with role-based permissions
  • Dedicated security team monitoring 24/7/365
  • DDoS protection and Web Application Firewall (WAF)

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in platform operations (payment processors, cloud hosting, KYC verification providers)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, subpoena, or legal process, including mining regulations
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales involving Hashvex
  • Protection of Rights: To investigate potential violations or protect against harm to our mining operations
  • Regulatory Reporting: With financial authorities as required by cryptocurrency regulations

Note: We never sell your personal information to third-party marketers or advertisers. Your mining data remains confidential.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your mining platform experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core mining platform functionality (session management, security)
  • Performance Cookies: Help analyze platform usage and mining performance metrics
  • Functional Cookies: Remember mining preferences and dashboard settings
  • Security Cookies: Detect malicious activity and protect against CSRF attacks
  • Analytics Cookies: Track mining efficiency and platform optimization opportunities

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies may affect mining platform functionality. Our platform requires certain cookies for secure mining operations.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information and mining records
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate mining account data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions (subject to regulatory retention requirements)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your mining information
  • Portability: Receive your mining data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities related to mining operations
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously granted permissions for data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at legal@hashvex.com. We may require identity verification before processing requests, especially for mining account-related inquiries.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, with specific consideration for mining operations:

  • Account Data: Retained while your mining account is active and for 7 years after closure for compliance with financial regulations
  • Financial Records: Kept for 10 years to meet tax, accounting, and cryptocurrency regulatory requirements
  • KYC Documents: Stored for the duration of your account plus 7 years as required by AML regulations
  • Technical Logs: Maintained for 2 years for security monitoring and mining performance analysis
  • Mining Performance Data: Retained for 5 years for operational optimization and historical analysis

Data may be retained longer when required by law, regulatory requirements, or for legitimate business needs related to Bitcoin mining operations.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Bitcoin mining platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete such information and terminate the associated mining account.

9. International Data Transfers

As a global Bitcoin mining platform, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for transfers to third countries
  • Adequacy decisions for transfers to approved jurisdictions
  • Additional technical and organizational safeguards for sensitive mining data
  • Data localization where required by cryptocurrency regulations

Our primary data processing occurs in jurisdictions with strong data protection frameworks, with mining operations distributed globally for optimal efficiency.

10. Mining-Specific Data Considerations

As a Bitcoin mining platform, we handle additional data types specific to mining operations:

  • Mining Hardware Data: Performance metrics, temperature readings, hash rates
  • Energy Consumption Data: Power usage statistics for mining operations
  • Blockchain Data: Public blockchain information related to mining activities
  • Pool Performance Data: Mining pool statistics and reward distributions

This mining-specific data is used exclusively for platform operations, performance optimization, and providing you with accurate mining statistics. We do not share individual mining performance data with third parties except as required for pool operations.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our mining operations or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify users via email for material changes affecting mining operations
  • Provide advance notice when required by cryptocurrency regulations
  • Obtain renewed consent when necessary for significant processing changes

Your continued use of our Bitcoin mining platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically, especially before making significant mining investments.
