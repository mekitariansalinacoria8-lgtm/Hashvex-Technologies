General Questions

What is Hashvex?

We combine cutting-edge mining technology with professional management services to deliver competitive returns on cryptocurrency investments.

Our platform is designed for serious investors seeking exposure to Bitcoin mining without the operational complexities of running their own facilities.

Yes, Hashvex is a fully registered and compliant cryptocurrency mining platform. We operate under Hashvex Technologies LLC, registered in Delaware, USA. Our operations are transparent, and we adhere to all applicable regulations in the jurisdictions where we operate.

Key indicators of our legitimacy include:

  • Registered business entity with verifiable documentation
  • Transparent terms of service and privacy policy
  • Physical mining facilities with verifiable locations
  • Professional customer support and responsive service
  • Positive track record with institutional partners

Account Questions

Why is KYC (Know Your Customer) required?

KYC verification is a standard security procedure required by financial regulations to prevent fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities. At Hashvex, we implement KYC to:

  • Protect our platform and users from fraudulent activities
  • Comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
  • Ensure the security of all transactions on our platform
  • Maintain the integrity of our mining operations

KYC is required for all accounts making purchases or rentals above $10,000 equivalent value.

What documents are accepted for KYC verification?

For KYC verification, we accept the following documents:

Identity Verification:

  • Passport (preferred)
  • Driver's license
  • National ID card

Address Verification:

  • Utility bill (electricity, water, gas) not older than 3 months
  • Bank statement
  • Government-issued tax bill

All documents must be clear, valid, and show your full name and residential address. Documents in languages other than English must be accompanied by a certified translation.

How long does KYC approval take?

Our standard KYC processing time is 1-3 business days after submission of all required documents. Processing may take longer during periods of high volume or if additional verification is required.

You can help expedite the process by:

  • Ensuring all documents are clear and legible
  • Providing complete documents (all corners visible, no cut-off information)
  • Making sure the name and address on all documents match exactly

You will receive email notifications about your KYC status throughout the verification process.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you've forgotten your password, follow these steps to reset it:

  1. Go to the Hashvex login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam folder if not received)
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours. If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

Security Tip: Always create a strong, unique password that you don't use for other services.

Transaction Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

Hashvex accepts the following payment methods for deposits and purchases:

Cryptocurrency Payments:

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Litecoin (LTC)
  • Bitcoin Cash (BCH)
  • Tether (USDT) - ERC20 and TRC20 networks

Fiat Currency Options:

  • Bank wire transfer (USD, EUR, GBP)
  • Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) - through our payment processors
  • SEPA transfers for European customers

All cryptocurrency payments require network confirmations before being credited to your account. The required confirmations vary by cryptocurrency:

  • BTC: 3 confirmations
  • ETH: 12 confirmations
  • LTC: 6 confirmations
  • BCH: 3 confirmations
  • USDT: 12 confirmations (ERC20), 6 confirmations (TRC20)
How long do deposits and withdrawals take to process?

Processing times vary depending on the payment method and network conditions:

Deposit Processing Times:

  • Cryptocurrency: 5-30 minutes after required network confirmations
  • Bank Wire: 1-3 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: Instant to 1 hour
  • SEPA Transfer: 1-2 business days

Withdrawal Processing Times:

  • Bitcoin Rewards: Processed within 24 hours (usually within 4 hours)
  • Bank Wire: 2-5 business days
  • SEPA Transfer: 1-3 business days

All withdrawals are subject to security review, which may add additional processing time for large or unusual transactions. You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the withdrawal process.

Are there any fees for deposits or withdrawals?

Hashvex aims to keep fees as low as possible. Our current fee structure is as follows:

Deposit Fees:

  • Cryptocurrency: Free (network fees paid by sender)
  • Bank Wire: 0.5% (min $25, max $250)
  • Credit/Debit Card: 3.5%
  • SEPA Transfer: 0.1% (min €10, max €100)

Withdrawal Fees:

  • Bitcoin Rewards: Network fee only (typically $2-5)
  • Bank Wire: 1% (min $30, max $300)
  • SEPA Transfer: 0.2% (min €15, max €150)

Mining Fees:

  • Pool Fee: 1% of mining rewards
  • Maintenance: Included in hosting fees for purchased miners
  • Electricity: $0.045-0.065 per kWh depending on location

All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction. We periodically review and update our fee structure to remain competitive.

Security Questions

How do you secure my funds and data?

At Hashvex, security is our top priority. We implement multiple layers of protection to safeguard your funds and personal information:

Fund Security:

  • 95% of digital assets held in cold storage (offline)
  • Multi-signature wallets for all hot wallets
  • Regular security audits by third-party firms
  • Insurance coverage for digital assets
  • Reserve fund to ensure liquidity

Data Protection:

  • Bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for all accounts
  • Regular penetration testing of our systems
  • Strict access controls and monitoring
  • GDPR-compliant data handling procedures

Account Security Features:

  • Login notifications
  • Withdrawal confirmation emails
  • Device management and recognition
  • Session timeouts
  • IP address monitoring

We continuously update our security measures to address emerging threats in the cryptocurrency space.

What security measures should I take for my account?

While we implement robust security measures on our end, you play a crucial role in keeping your account secure. Here are our top recommendations:

Essential Security Practices:

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) rather than SMS when possible
  • Use a Strong, Unique Password: At least 12 characters with mixed cases, numbers, and symbols
  • Beware of Phishing: Always check URLs before entering credentials; we'll never ask for your password via email
  • Secure Your Email: Use 2FA on your email account as it's often the weakest link
  • Monitor Account Activity: Regularly review login history and withdrawal activity

Advanced Security Measures:

  • Use a dedicated device for cryptocurrency transactions
  • Consider using a password manager
  • Whitelist withdrawal addresses where possible
  • Set up withdrawal limits and notifications
  • Keep your devices and browsers updated

If you suspect any unauthorized access to your account, immediately change your password, revoke any active sessions through your account settings, and contact our security team.
