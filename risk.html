Enterprise Risk Disclosure Statement

This document outlines the substantial risks associated with enterprise Bitcoin mining investment through Hashvex Technologies LLC. By using our platform, you acknowledge and accept these risks in their entirety.

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Hashvex Technologies LLC operates a real-time, enterprise-grade Bitcoin mining platform for institutional and retail investors. Cryptocurrency investments carry substantial risk of capital loss. This disclosure does not purport to disclose all risks associated with our services. You should carefully consider whether cryptocurrency investment is appropriate for you based on your financial circumstances and risk tolerance.

Warning: By accessing Hashvex services, you confirm that you understand and accept all risks outlined herein. You alone assume responsibility for all investment decisions made through your account.

2. Bitcoin Market Volatility

Bitcoin markets are highly volatile and can fluctuate dramatically in short periods due to:

  • Changes in market demand and liquidity
  • Regulatory developments and government actions
  • Technological advancements or failures in blockchain networks
  • Macroeconomic conditions and geopolitical events
  • Market manipulation and trading anomalies
  • Network difficulty adjustments

Historical performance is not indicative of future results. The value of Bitcoin may decrease precipitously and without warning. Hashvex does not guarantee returns or principal protection on mining investments.

3. Loss of Capital

3.1 Investment Risk

All mining investments through Hashvex carry the risk of complete loss of capital. You should only invest funds you can afford to lose entirely. Factors that may contribute to loss include:

  • Adverse Bitcoin price movements
  • Operational issues with mining equipment
  • Rapid increases in mining difficulty
  • Network congestion and fee fluctuations
  • Platform performance factors
  • Electricity cost volatility

3.2 Mining-Specific Risks

Bitcoin mining investments are subject to additional risks:

  • Hash rate volatility and equipment efficiency degradation
  • ASIC miner obsolescence (12-24 month lifecycle)
  • Power cost fluctuations in mining facilities
  • Network difficulty adjustments (every 2016 blocks)
  • Mining pool performance variations
  • Hardware failure rates and maintenance costs

Note: Hashvex's projected returns are estimates based on current Bitcoin price, network difficulty, and electricity costs. These are not guaranteed and may vary significantly.

4. Technology Risk

Bitcoin mining systems rely on complex technologies that may fail or be compromised:

4.1 System Vulnerabilities

Potential technological risks include:

  • ASIC miner firmware bugs or hardware defects
  • Mining pool software vulnerabilities
  • Cybersecurity breaches at facility level
  • Network connectivity and latency issues
  • Hardware failures in mining operations (3-5% annual failure rate typical)
  • Cooling system failures in mining facilities

4.2 Hashvex Security Measures

Hashvex implements enterprise-grade security protocols including:

  • Multi-signature wallets for Bitcoin storage
  • Cold storage for majority of assets
  • DDoS protection and WAF implementation
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • Encrypted communications and data storage
  • 24/7 network monitoring and SOC2 compliance

No security system is impenetrable. You acknowledge that technological failures may result in loss of funds.

5. Regulatory Risk

Cryptocurrency regulations vary significantly by jurisdiction and may change unpredictably:

5.1 Legal Uncertainty

Potential regulatory impacts include:

  • Changes in tax treatment of Bitcoin mining income
  • Restrictions or bans on cryptocurrency mining operations
  • Licensing requirements affecting platform availability
  • Forced liquidation or freezing of assets by authorities
  • Energy consumption regulations affecting mining operations

5.2 User Responsibility

You are solely responsible for:

  • Complying with local laws and regulations regarding cryptocurrency
  • Reporting taxable events from mining income
  • Understanding legal status of Bitcoin mining in your jurisdiction
  • Any penalties or legal actions resulting from non-compliance

Important: Hashvex may restrict services or freeze accounts to comply with applicable laws, without liability for resulting losses. Hashvex operates under Delaware LLC regulations and complies with US federal guidelines.

6. Platform & Backend Dependency

Hashvex operations depend on complex technical infrastructure:

6.1 Service Interruptions

Potential service impacts include:

  • Scheduled maintenance windows (notified in advance)
  • Unscheduled downtime due to technical issues
  • Third-party service failures (hosting, APIs, payment processors)
  • Network connectivity issues between mining facilities
  • Power grid instability in mining facility locations

Note: While Hashvex implements redundancy measures across multiple facilities, we cannot guarantee uninterrupted service availability and are not liable for losses due to temporary outages. Our Service Level Agreement targets 99.5% uptime.

7. No Financial Advice

Hashvex provides execution-only mining services:

  • We do not provide investment, tax, or legal advice
  • All investment decisions are made at your sole discretion
  • Information on our platform should not be construed as recommendations
  • You should consult licensed financial advisors before making investment decisions
  • Mining profitability calculators are estimates only

Warning: Past performance is not indicative of future results. Bitcoin mining investments may result in loss of principal. Mining difficulty has historically increased over time, reducing profitability for existing hardware.

8. KYC and Risk Assessment

8.1 Verification Requirements

Hashvex conducts mandatory Know Your Customer (KYC) verification:

  • All users must complete identity verification (tiered system)
  • Enhanced due diligence for high-value accounts
  • Ongoing transaction monitoring for AML compliance
  • Risk scoring based on multiple factors including jurisdiction and transaction patterns
  • Source of funds verification for large deposits

8.2 Account Restrictions

We may impose limitations including:

  • Tiered deposit/withdrawal limits based on verification level
  • Investment amount caps for new users
  • Temporary holds for suspicious activity review
  • Account closures when warranted by compliance requirements
  • Jurisdiction-based restrictions where required by law

9. User Responsibility

You bear full responsibility for:

9.1 Account Security

Essential security measures:

  • Enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) - mandatory
  • Using strong, unique passwords and password manager
  • Keeping login credentials and API keys confidential
  • Monitoring account activity regularly for unauthorized access
  • Secure storage of backup codes and recovery keys

9.2 Investment Decisions

You must make informed decisions regarding:

  • Selection of mining hardware based on current market conditions
  • Understanding of mining contracts and fee structures
  • Risk tolerance assessment for volatile Bitcoin market
  • Diversification strategy across multiple miners or durations
  • Exit strategy planning for mining investments

Important: Hashvex is not responsible for losses due to user error, including incorrect transaction details, poor investment timing, or unauthorized access resulting from negligence in account security.
